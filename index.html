Escape From Tarkov Hideout Guide

What's new?

Since the 2021 wipe, Battlestate Games has been silently tweaking and changing Hideout requirements, dealer barter trades, and presumably drop rates. All the information on this site is as up-to-date as I could find. Below, I have a list of changes that were made:

  • [Rest Space 2] The 2nd tier now requires additional Energy-saving lamps, a DVD drive, and a magnet. The ruble cost and reputation requirements are unchanged.
  • [Nutrition Unit 3] Now requires 2 "Chimney" cleaner. On a side note, I don't think I've ever seen one of these yet this wipe...
  • [Generator 1] (Updated 2021-01-13) Now requires a spark plug, not a car battery.
  • [Water Collector 2] / [Lavatory 2] Both now require Kek tape.
  • [Lavatory 1] Now requires 1 toilet paper.
  • [Heating 2] Now requires 3 dry fuel, 2 hunter matches, and Vents 2.
  • [Vents 2] Now requires 3 CPU fans.
  • [Solar Power] Now requires 10,000 additional Euros (up to 25,000), 4 more military cable and 4 more military power filter, and 2 more phased array elements. This basically doubles the cost of the solar power station; it still performs the same function of reducing fuel usage by 50%.
  • [Air Filtering Unit] Now requires 2 "air filter for gas mask" and one military power filter. Other requirements remain the same.
  • [Scav Case] Costs went way up - requires 2 additional bronze lions (for a total of 4), 4 additional gold skull rings (for a total of 6), 2 more golden neck chains (for a total of 8) and 1 additional "Roler" gold wrist watch (for a total of 4).
  • [Library] Used to only require rubles, now also requires 2 chainlets and 1 horse figurine.
  • [Stash 4] No longer requires Intel Center 3.

Did I miss anything? Send me a DM on Reddit or Tweet at me.

About

EFT's Hideout feature lets players spend resources, like items found in a raid or money, in order to upgrade stations. Stations like the medbay, workshop, and nutrition unit all allow players a chance to craft items. Other stations, like the booze generator or scav case, reward PMCs with items over time. See more information on our about page.
