Escape From Tarkov Hideout Guide

What's new?

Since the 2021 wipe, Battlestate Games has been silently tweaking and changing Hideout requirements, dealer barter trades, and presumably drop rates. All the information on this site is as up-to-date as I could find. Below, I have a list of changes that were made:

  • Added car battery as a requirement to build the tier 1 Generator. While not strictly a requirement for much (only the Nutrition Unit requires it to be built), the Generator is required to do a lot of the crafting newer players need, including most Medstation crafts.
  • Rest Space tier 2 now requires additional Energy-saving lamps, a DVD drive, and a magnet. The ruble cost and reputation requirements are unchanged.

Did I miss anything? Send me a DM on Reddit or Tweet at me.

About

Escape From Tarkov's "Hideout" feature lets players spend resources, like items found in a raid or money, in order to upgrade stations. Stations like the medbay, workshop, and nutrition unit all allow players a chance to craft items. Other stations, like the booze generator or scav case, reward PMCs with items over time. See more information on our about page.

This guide can help you understand what the costs are for each station & which prerequisites are needed. Try interacting with and exploring the graph to optimize your upgrades!
