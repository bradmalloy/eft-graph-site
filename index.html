About — Escape From Tarkov Hideout Guide

Around January 12th, 2021, the car battery was added as a requirement to build the Generator. Unless drop rates are also modified, this significantly increases the cost of getting your hideout started.

Escape From Tarkov's "Hideout" feature lets players spend resources, like items found in a raid or money, in order to upgrade stations. Stations like the medbay, workshop, and nutrition unit all allow players a chance to craft items. Other stations, like the booze generator or scav case, reward PMCs with items over time. See more information on our about page.

This guide can help you understand what the costs are for each station & which prerequisites are needed. Try interacting with and exploring the graph to optimize your upgrades!
